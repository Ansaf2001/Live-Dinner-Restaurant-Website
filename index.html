Live Dinner’s, though not a small business, has faced some trough patches throughout their history, the most recent being with their pizza and burger tasting like cardboard. To address this issue and how they turned their foods around, Live Dinner’s released this article telling the story as to how they fixed their foods. brings the viewer behind the scenes to their test kitchen where the chefs and company executives are interviewed and questioned about their terrible foods. The story begins with focus groups and negative reviews the company had to completely redefining their traditional foods in their test kitchens. Live Dinner’s reintroduces their brand to the public in this storytelling article and redefines their business for the future.
